About Me

Katie McCarthy is a classical musician and fullstack web developer (nerd alert) living the dream in Chicago! You can find her quizzing her private horn studio students on their scales (they love that), teaching group french horn lessons at Lincoln Park High School, performing with her genre-mixing brass quintet Temperance Brass, as well as building and maintaining websites for freelance clients. If she's got some time to herself, she's likely walking along a boulevard in her Logan Square hood, coffee in hand, petting every single dog she sees.
